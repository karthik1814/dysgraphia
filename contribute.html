Our solution runs across three broad categories of symptoms of Dysgraphia.


Letter and Writing

Symptoms include messy handwriting, Reverse Numbers, writing backwards, and mixing up Lowercase and Uppercase. This features helps us understand the writing proficiency of your child.

Writing Conventions

Symptoms include irregular spacing, writing in lines and margin, writing from left to right orientation among others. This will help us understand the writing proficiency of your child.

Writing Proficiency

Symptoms of Writing Proficiency include resist tasks, getting difficulty on copying texts, spelling errors among others. This helps us understand proficiency in the handwriting of your child.
