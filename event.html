Our Events

Dysgraphia is a challenging condition that can cause tremendous emotional hardship and distress in both children and adults. Dysgraphia is a condition in which a person has trouble spelling written words or writing at a normal speed, but not necessarily reading or speaking. We've paired together with psychologists, educators, and professionals to host seminars aimed at raising awareness regarding Dysgraphia's nuances.


The main objective of conducting the events is to raise awareness about dysgraphia, its challenges, and support strategies among the students and faculty of IIIT Kota. A renowned expert in the field of learning disorders, such as dysgraphia, was invited as a guest speaker. The speaker shared insights, research findings, and practical approaches to understanding and managing dysgraphia effectively.


These events include informative presentations and interactive workshops to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of dysgraphia. These sessions covered topics such as the definition and symptoms of dysgraphia, its impact on individuals' academic performance, and practical strategies for intervention. Real-life case studies and personal experiences of individuals with dysgraphia were shared to provide firsthand insights into the challenges they face. This helped participants develop empathy and a deeper understanding of the emotional and academic difficulties associated with dysgraphia.
